Manifesto

BUILD is a creative set-building workshop, founded by a team of experienced builders, decorators, and set designers with over 15 years of expertise in fashion, art, and events.

Based in Paris, we cater to international clients, delivering tailor-made projects that consistently exceed expectations. Every creation at BUILD is an opportunity to craft something exceptional and closely aligned with our clients' vision.
